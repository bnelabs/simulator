What is "Reliability Score"?

This score measures how closely the machine is following its "Golden Baseline" (the signature of the machine when it was new/healthy).

85 - 100%

OPTIMAL: Machine is running as designed. No action needed.

50 - 85%

DEGRADED: Early signs of wear. Schedule inspection within 14 days.

Below 50%

CRITICAL: Structural damage occurring. Stop machine to prevent total loss.
